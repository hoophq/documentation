**Hoop.dev is a secure access gateway for databases and servers.** It sits between your users and your infrastructure, enforcing policies and masking sensitive data without complex rule setup.

## The Challenge of Infrastructure Access

Managing access to critical infrastructure creates real problems:

- Overly permissive policies create security risks
- Complex approval workflows slow down developers
- Inadequate audit trails block compliance
- No visibility into who accessed what and when
- Sensitive data exposed during access sessions
